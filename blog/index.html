'Practise, patience, and persistence' is Arinze Stanley Egbe motto.

"I actually never attended any art classes till today. I just kept practising so i guess thats the power of inspiration," he said. Arinze Stanley Egbe is a nigerian hyperrealism artist who got inspired and motivated deeply by works of Kelvin Odanz and Emmanuele Dascanio. He has carved a niche for himself as one of the few sucessful nigerian artist in the genre of hyperrealism.

The indian festival of colors.

Holi has been celebrated in the indians subcontinent for centuries. It marks the beginning of spring after a long winter, symbolic of the triumph of the good over evil. It is celebrated in march, corresponding to the Hindu calender month of phalguna.
